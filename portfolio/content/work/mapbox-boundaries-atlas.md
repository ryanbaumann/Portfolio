## The goal

As Mapbox's first customer-facing engineer, I hit the same two blockers in enterprise deals: customers required administrative boundary data for data visualization, and regulated customers refused to use cloud-hosted maps. Each blocker represented a missing core product.

## What shipped

I drove both from zero to one as the founding product manager:

- **Boundaries** — I built and launched a global administrative boundary data product for joining customer data to geography, leading engineering and UX through strategy and execution.
- **Atlas** — I shipped self-hosted Mapbox for on-premises and air-gapped environments, immediately unlocking the government and financial services segments.

I drove both products to their first $5M ARR before handing them off to dedicated PMs. Both remain in Mapbox's core portfolio 8+ years later.

## Why it matters

The highest-signal product insights come directly from stalled deals. Converting field friction into durable platform products — and hard revenue — is the exact pattern I have executed at every scale since.
